OUR STORY

A kitchen built
around the market, not the menu.

OLIVA started as a single wood oven and a market basket. Every morning our chefs walk the Taza Bazar before deciding what the day's menu will be — which is why our dishes change with the seasons, not with trends.

No shortcuts, no freezers full of pre-made sauces. Just good ingredients, treated simply, served the way we'd want to eat at home.

— Rauf Aliyev, Head Chef & Founder
